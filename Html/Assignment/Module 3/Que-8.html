What are the various formatting tags in HTML ?


Ans -> Formatting tag is used to give the different types of format to text.
            There are many types of Formatting tag.

            Example : <h1> </h1> to <h6> </h6> (It gives hight to the text in order of high to low.)

                               <b></b> , <strong></strong> (It used to give bold style to the text )

                               <i></i> , <em></em> , <cite></cite> (It used to give Italic style to the text.)

                               <u></u> , <ins></ins> (It used to give underline to the texts)

                               <big></big> , <small></small> (Big tag gives text to a larger than normal size & small tag used to give text smaller than normal size.)

                               <p></p> , <pre></pre> (p tag used for paragraph & pre tag shows the data as it is.)

                               <mark><</mark> (It highlights the texts.)

                               <del></del> , <strike></strike> (del/strike tag used to cut the texts.)

                               <code></code> (It gives plain text style to the texts.)

                               <q><q> , <blockquote></blockquote> (q tag put text in the "" & blockquote put space around the texts.)

                               <br> (It breaks the line.)

                               <hr> (It used to draw Horizontal line.)

                               <abbr> (It used to give information about texts when hover the cursor. )
